About Us


The bakers in our bakery sells exclusive and indulgent range of offerings that include brownies, cakes, desserts and breads.

From our humble beginnings in 2004, when the first Bakers store opened its doors at the iconic Cusrow Baug at Colaba Causeway (Mumbai), we've grown to become a Pan-India chain of patisseries with stores in over 18 cities.

Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate, and this journey continues as we open Bakers House across the country.
